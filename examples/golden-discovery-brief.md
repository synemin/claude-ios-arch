# Golden Example: Discovery Brief

## Problem Statement
People want a low-friction AI journaling app that helps them reflect daily without forcing a long writing habit.

## Target User
Busy professionals who want a lightweight reflection habit and are willing to use guided prompts on mobile.

## Core Use Cases
- capture a short daily reflection in under 2 minutes
- receive a useful guided prompt
- revisit prior entries without friction

## MVP Boundary
- one primary journaling flow
- lightweight onboarding
- local-first storage with optional account later
- no social features
- no advanced analytics dashboard

## Key Risks
- first value may take too long if onboarding is too heavy
- AI quality may not justify retention if prompts are weak
- privacy expectations are high for journaling products
- App Store review risk if metadata overstates AI capabilities

## App Store / Policy Sensitivities
- privacy messaging must be clear
- any AI claims should be accurate and not misleading
- if account creation exists, deletion path should be planned early

## Recommended Next Step
Validate first-value flow and privacy posture with a single vertical prototype before committing to broader architecture.
