# End-to-End Example: Professional Developer Journey

## Discovery
Use `ios-discovery` to align product scope, policy risks, and MVP shape with stakeholders.

## Development
Use:
- `ios-app-bootstrap` for architecture baseline
- `ios-feature-bootstrap` for feature decomposition
- `ios-testing-strategy` for quality plan
- `ios-tooling-decision` for engineering tool adoption
- `ios-arch-review` and `ios-debt-check` during growth

## Release & Operations
Use `ios-release-operations` for beta, submission readiness, and launch preparation.

## Principle
Bias toward maintainability, observability, and delivery speed without over-engineering early.
