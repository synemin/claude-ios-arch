# App Store Risk Screen

Use this screen in discovery and release workflows.

## Common Risk Areas
- subscriptions / IAP flows
- user-generated content and moderation
- AI-generated content claims and safety
- login / account deletion compliance
- camera / microphone / location permissions
- health / finance / children / sensitive data
- misleading metadata or screenshots

## Questions
- Does the app need special permissions? Why?
- Will App Review understand the value and behavior quickly?
- Are there risky claims in metadata or onboarding?
- Does the app collect data that must be disclosed clearly?
- Does the app require content moderation or abuse handling?
